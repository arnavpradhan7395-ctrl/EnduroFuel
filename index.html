Zone Carb Demand

Why 5g/kg carbs on endurance days
Glycogen is the primary substrate above ~60% VO2max. Each hour at Z3–Z4 burns ~60g glycogen. Under-fuelling impairs power output, elevates cortisol, and slows aerobic adaptation — even on a fat-loss programme.
